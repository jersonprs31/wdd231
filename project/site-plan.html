Site Plan: Guatemala Antojitos

1. Site Name

Name: Guatemala Antojitos

Reason: The name directly communicates the focus of the website, which is sharing and exploring traditional street food, snacks, and appetizers (antojitos) from Guatemala.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to celebrate and catalog authentic Guatemalan cuisine. It provides users with a directory of popular dishes like garnachas, rellenitos, and chuchitos, including their history, regional origins, and traditional ingredients. It serves as an educational and culinary resource for food enthusiasts wanting to learn about Guatemalan gastronomy.

3. Scenarios

These questions represent the needs of our target audience:

  • What are the essential ingredients needed to make authentic Guatemalan garnachas at home?
  • What is the history behind sweet plantain rellenitos and how are they traditionally served?

4. Color Schema

The following colors will be used to reflect the culture and culinary elements of Guatemala:

  • Primary Color (#00416a - Deep Blue): Represents the Guatemalan flag. Used for the header, footer, modal dialogs, and main headings.
  • Secondary Color (#b34728 - Terracotta): Represents traditional clay cookware and roasted spices. Used for buttons, borders, and interactive links.
  • Background Color (#fdfbf7 - Warm Cream): Represents corn masa, a staple in Guatemalan cuisine. Used as the main body background to keep the site warm and readable.
  • Text Color (#2c2c2c - Charcoal): Used for all general paragraph and body text to ensure maximum readability and accessibility.

5. Typography

Font Family: 'Roboto', Arial, sans-serif.

Application: This sans-serif font will be applied universally across the site for both headings and body text. It is clean, modern, and highly legible, making it perfect for reading recipes and cultural information on both mobile and desktop screens.
